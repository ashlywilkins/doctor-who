Peter Davison

Peter Davison (born Peter Malcolm Gordon Moffett on 13 April 1951)[1] is an English actor, best known for his roles as Tristan Farnon in the television version of James Herriot's All Creatures Great and Small, and as the fifth doctor in Doctor Who, which he played from 1981 to 1984.[2] Also, he played David Braithwaite in At Home with the Braithwaites. Since 2011 he has been playing Henry Sharpe in Law & Order: UK.
