Billie Piper

Her most famous role is as Rose Tyler, companion to The Doctor from 2005 to 2006, in the BBC sci-fi seriesDoctor Who, a role she reprised in 2008,[3] 2010, and 2013.[4] In 2007,Broadcast magazine listed Piper at no. 6 in its "Hot 100" list of influential on-screen performers; she was the top woman on the list.[5][6] From 2007 until 2011, she starred as the high-flying escort Belle de Jour in the TV seriesSecret Diary of a Call Girl. She currently stars as Brona, an Irish immigrant, in the horror TV series Penny Dreadful.
