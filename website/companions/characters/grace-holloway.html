Grace Holloway

Dr. Grace Holloway is a fictional character played by Daphne Ashbrook in the 1996 television movie Doctor Who, a continuation of the long-running British science fiction television series Doctor Who. A cardiologist from 1999San Francisco, she assisted the Eighth Doctor against the renegade Time Lord known as the Master.
